# updatePurchaseNeeds

## Purpose
Updates the purchase needs tab based on current inventory and assignment data.

## How it Works
- Analyzes inventory and assignment data.
- Calculates items that need to be purchased.
- Updates the purchase needs tab accordingly.

## Example Usage
Called during inventory review or when new assignments are made.

## Inputs
None.

## Outputs
Purchase needs tab updated.

## Side Effects
May trigger notifications or further actions.
